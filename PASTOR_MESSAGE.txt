WHAT THIS SYSTEM OFFERS:

1. MEMBER & FAMILY MANAGEMENT
   • Register individual members or entire families at once
   • Track marital status and life status (Alive/Deceased)
   • Organize families with head of family and relationships
   • View families as grouped units with expandable details
   • Search and filter members easily

2. ATTENDANCE TRACKING
   • Record weekly service attendance
   • Maintain attendance history
   • Export data for reports

3. FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT
   • Record tithes and offerings
   • Track expenses
   • Generate financial summaries

4. SERMON & BIBLE STUDY RECORDS
   • Document sermon details (date, title, preacher, Bible text)
   • Store sermon media links
   • Track Bible study sessions

5. DEPARTMENT/MINISTRY MANAGEMENT
   • Organize members by departments
   • Track ministry assignments
   • Manage department rosters

6. BIRTHDAY TRACKING
   • View upcoming birthdays automatically
   • Never miss a member's special day

7. MESSAGING SYSTEM
   • Send SMS messages to members (recommended for Nigeria)
   • Send email notifications
   • Choose recipients: all members, active only, specific members, or specific families
   • Perfect for announcements, reminders, and updates

8. MEMBER/FAMILY OF THE WEEK
   • System automatically selects one member or family each week
   • Ensures fair rotation - no repeats until all are selected
   • Send weekly prayer messages with one click
   • Ideal for focused pastoral prayer and encouragement

9. AUDIT LOGS
   • Complete activity tracking (who did what and when)
   • Security and accountability
   • Review all system changes and activities

10. MOBILE ACCESSIBILITY
   • Fully functional on smartphones and tablets
   • Can be installed as an app on your phone
   • Works offline when needed
   • Access from anywhere, anytime

11. DATA EXPORT
   • Export all data to CSV format
   • Easy backup and reporting
   • Compatible with Excel and other systems
